Code for multivariate and univariate analysis of behavioral variables to generate figures 2, S3, S4 AND S5

Used in the article: Oliveira-Junior et al. Multidimensional behavioral profiles associated with resilience and susceptibility after inescapable stress. Scientific Reports (2024).

OBS.: To run this script, you must use the data from the 'Data_Table.xlsx' file, available at: https://osf.io/mgknc, and add the 'utilities_script2' folder to the path.

Authors: Benedito Alves de Oliveira-Júnior, Danilo Benette Marques & Rafael Naime Ruggiero (2019-2024).

Institution: Department of Neurosciences and Behavioral Sciences, Ribeirão Preto Medical School, University of São Paulo.
